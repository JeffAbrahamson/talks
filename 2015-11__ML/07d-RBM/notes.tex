\notetitle{0x7d}{Restricted Boltzmann Machines}

Convolution:
\begin{displaymath}
  f(t)\ast g(t) = (f\ast g)(t) \vcentcolon = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} f(\tau) g(t-\tau) d\tau
\end{displaymath}

Examples:
\begin{itemize}
\item Sliding windows
\item Rolling mean
\end{itemize}
